  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    1. Accuracy Human errors are the most common in the traditional attendance management system. False attendance marking can even affect the future of a student. An online attendance system for schools ensures accurate records and eliminates human errors. This precise data helps teachers to manage attendance efficiently.
    2. Cost-effective An online attendance management tool can save you money in many ways. Since it is entirely digital, you can save the money spent on heaps of paperwork. You can also save time, money, and resources lost due to human errors in manual data entry.
    3. Efficiency Managing the attendance of students is time-consuming, expensive, and laborious. It takes time to process paperwork and generate reports manually. You can easily save your precious time by using an online attendance system for schools. The system keeps track of student hours and generates reports automatically.
    4. Insights The online attendance system for schools allows you to generate valuable insights with just a few clicks. For example, you can obtain month-wise, class-wise, or subject-wise reports for deeper analysis. The software can generate and store any customized reports in a single location. Teachers can make use of this data to maintain a precise attendance management process.
    5. Smooth Workflow An online attendance management system for schools ensures a smooth workflow. The system automatically sends instant notifications and alerts to the parents whenever a student is absent. So, schools don't have to call or notify parents as in the traditional method. Schools can also manage class schedules, shifts of teachers, student details, etc., from a single platform.
    6. Flexibility In today's digital environment, teachers and students attend classes from different locations having different time zones. An online attendance management tool allows you to track attendance using different clocking options. The software uses technologies like biometric terminals, desktop readers, etc.
    7. Configurable The online attendance systems for schools are configurable. It means that you can integrate the system with third-party apps to meet your needs. For example, the system allows you to use biometrics to track the attendance of the students. You can also generate customized attendance policies according to your needs.
    8. Real-time Tracking Another important benefit of an online attendance management system is real-time tracking. The system works on the cloud, which means teachers can monitor students in real-time. Teachers will get instant notifications whenever students take a break or miss a class.
    9. Security Attendance management is a process that deals with sensitive, personal details of individuals. The hard copy data is not safe and is prone to theft in the traditional method. An online attendance management systems is highly secure. It stores the personal data safely in a single location. Only an authorised staff can access the data.
